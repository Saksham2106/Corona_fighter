The Great Corona Combanant

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Hover on MOVE button to move forward towards gem and remove the cursor from MOVE button to STOP the player.

  • Health Score is increased by 10 every pixel player moves

    • score = score + 10
  • If player and corona virus collide your score is decreased by 100

    • score = score - 100
  • You lose if at any point of time during the game, the player score becomes negative

  • Default game mode is HARD. By decreasing game level, you have a better chance to live :)
